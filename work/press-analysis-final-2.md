| Tier | Points | Examples |

| **Regional/Local** | 15 | Adevarul, Gazeta Nord-Vest, local papers |

### Factor 2: Coverage Depth (0-40 points)

| Type | Points | Description |
|------|--------|-------------|
| **Dedicated Article** | 40 | Entire article about Yayzy/Cristian |
| **Dedicated Interview** | 40 | Q&A or interview format |
| **Major Section** | 30 | Dedicated paragraph(s) with details |
| **Featured in List (Top 3)** | 25 | #1-3 in a "top X" list |
| **Featured in List (4+)** | 20 | Listed in "top X" but lower position |
| **Brief Mention** | 10 | Name-dropped, 1-2 sentences |

### Factor 3: Personal Brand Boost (0-20 points)

| Type | Points | Description |
|------|--------|-------------|
| **Cristian interviewed/quoted** | 20 | Direct quotes or interview |
| **Cristian named as CTO/founder** | 15 | Explicitly credits Cristian |
| **Team mentioned** | 10 | "Co-founders" or team reference |
| **Company only** | 5 | Just Yayzy mentioned |
